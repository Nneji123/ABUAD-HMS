The ABUAD hostel monitoring system is an innovative application that helps to detect and prevent violence and smoking in the hostel environments of Afe Babalola University, Ado-Ekiti (ABUAD). The system provides a range of features to support the safety and well-being of students, faculty, and staff at the university.

Some of the key features of the ABUAD hostel monitoring system include:

  • Automated detection of violent behavior through audio and video analysis.
  • Real-time monitoring of smoking in hostel rooms through smoke detectors and video analysis.
  • Detailed reporting and analytics on hostel activity and incidents.
