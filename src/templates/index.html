What is ABUAD Hostel Monitoring System?

The ABUAD Hostel Monitoring System is an innovative application that helps to detect and prevent violence and smoking in the hostel environments of Afe Babalola University, Ado-Ekiti (ABUAD). The system provides a range of features to support the safety and well-being of students, faculty, and staff at the university.

Automated Detection of Violence

The ABUAD Hostel Monitoring System uses audio and video analysis to automatically detect violent behavior in the hostel environment, enabling early intervention to prevent incidents and ensure the safety of all occupants.

Real-time Monitoring of Smoking

The ABUAD Hostel Monitoring System monitors smoking in hostel rooms in real-time using smoke detectors and video analysis, helping to enforce the university's no-smoking policy and create a healthier living environment for all occupants.

Detailed Reporting and Analytics

The ABUAD Hostel Monitoring System provides detailed reporting and analytics on hostel activity and incidents, allowing university administrators to identify trends and take proactive measures to prevent future incidents.
